Module 1 · Challenge

Mining Remains One of India's
Most Dangerous Workplaces

Between 2020–2024, India recorded 195 fatal and 726 serious mining accidents — a major accident every two days. The same root causes repeat, year after year.

💨

Toxic Gas Exposure

Deadly levels of CO, CO₂, and methane build up silently underground. Without real-time detection, miners have no warning until it's too late.

⚠️

No Real-Time Monitoring

Health vitals like heart rate and oxygen go unchecked. Fatigue and distress accumulate with no supervisor awareness deep in the shaft.

🏥

Delayed Rescue Operations

Falls and collapses leave miners incapacitated with no way to call for help. Without location data, rescue teams waste critical minutes searching.

💡

Why a Smart Solution Is Needed

Traditional helmets provide only physical protection. They do nothing to detect invisible threats — gas, vitals, or a miner who has fallen unconscious in an unreachable corner of a deep shaft. Mine Sense turns the helmet into an active guardian: always sensing, always connected, and always ready to raise the alarm.

Module 2 · Innovation

What Mine Sense Delivers

Seven intelligent sensing capabilities — every one critical for underground survival.

🌫️

Gas Detection

MQ-135 Sensor

Continuously monitors CO, CO₂, ammonia, and other toxic gases. Buzzer fires instantly when concentration crosses the danger threshold.

Critical Safety
❤️

Heart Rate & SpO₂

MAX30102 Sensor

Optical pulse and oxygen saturation tracking. Detects stress, fatigue, or cardiac events and triggers an immediate alert if readings are abnormal.

Health Monitoring
🧭

Fall & Movement Detection

MPU6050 Sensor

6-axis accelerometer and gyroscope detect falls, sudden impacts, and abnormal head tilt. Automatic SOS triggered on fall detection.

Accident Detection
🌡️

Temp & Humidity

DHT11 Sensor

Monitors ambient temperature and humidity levels to ensure environmental safety and alert supervisors before conditions become dangerous.

Environment
📍

GPS Tracking

GPS Module (NEO-6M)

Real-time miner location transmitted to supervisor dashboard. Emergency GPS coordinates shared immediately via LoRa on SOS activation.

Location Safety
📡

LoRa Wireless Comm.

Ra-02 LoRa Module

Long-range, low-power wireless data transmission. Works inside deep mine shafts where conventional WiFi and Bluetooth are completely blocked.

Communication
🚨

Emergency Alert System

Buzzer + Touch SOS + Reed Switch

Multi-layer alert system: local buzzer warns the miner, manual SOS button triggers rescue signal, reed switch detects if helmet is removed. All states relayed instantly to the control room with GPS location and vitals.

Emergency SOS
Module 3 · Technical Design
Module 3 · Architecture

System Architecture

A five-layer IoT pipeline — from sensors on the helmet to the supervisor's screen.

01
🔌

Sensing Layer

MQ135, DHT11, MPU6050, MAX30102, GPS, Touch, Reed Switch collect environmental and health data continuously.

↓
02
⚙️

Processing Layer

Arduino / ESP32 filters sensor data, checks safety thresholds, identifies anomalies, and prepares data packets for transmission.

↓
03
📡

Communication Layer

Ra-02 LoRa module transmits encrypted data packets wirelessly over several kilometres at low power consumption.

↓
04
🖥️

Monitoring Layer

Supervisor dashboard displays live sensor readings, GPS map, miner status, and historical graphs. I2C LCD on the helmet shows local readings.

↓
05
🔔

Alert & Safety Layer

Local buzzer + vibration alerts the miner. Remote alert messages with GPS coordinates are sent to initiate rescue operations immediately.

⚡ Real-time 🔋 Low Power 📶 Multi-km Range 🛡 Fail-safe
Module 5 · Results & Impact

Results & Achievements

During testing, Mine Sense delivered reliable performance across all core functions.

✅

Accurate Fall Detection

MPU6050 reliably detected falls and abnormal head movements, triggering SOS within milliseconds.

✅

Real-Time Alerts

Any dangerous gas level, abnormal heart rate, or fall triggered alerts within seconds — locally and remotely.

✅

Stable LoRa Communication

After a month of debugging, LoRa transmission became stable and reliable over the tested range.

✅

Clear LCD Output

Live sensor readings displayed on the helmet-mounted I2C LCD — readable even in dim mine lighting.

✅

Reliable Heart Rate Data

MAX30102 provided consistent pulse and SpO₂ readings throughout extended test sessions.

✅

Fast Gas Response

MQ135 responded rapidly to simulated gas conditions, with the buzzer firing well within the safety window.
